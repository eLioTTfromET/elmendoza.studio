Meet the band

Robert, flute & heavy breathing

Picture this: ethereal flute melodies floating delicately above a backdrop of intense, rhythmic breaths. It's a sonic experience unlike any other. I'm all about pushing boundaries and breaking norms. Who says heavy breathing can't be music? In my hands (and lungs), it becomes part of the composition, adding layers of depth and emotion.
